Tribute to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, the Missile Man of India, was a renowned scientist and the 11th President of India. He made significant contributions to India's space and missile programs, and he was a great teacher and mentor to many. His life and work continue to inspire people around the world.

He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, and passed away on July 27, 2015. Dr. Kalam's vision and dedication to education and science left a lasting impact on the nation.

Let us remember and honor his legacy by striving for excellence and working towards a better future for our country and the world.
